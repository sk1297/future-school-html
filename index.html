Who We Are

About Future School

Established in 2016, Future Pre-Primary School is proudly Dhule's first and most advanced digital pre-primary school, located at 94, Satya Sai Baba Society, Sakri Road, Dhule. We were founded with a single, powerful vision — to give every child in Dhule access to world-class early education that is smart, safe, and joyful.

Over the past 10+ years, we have nurtured 500+ students from Play Group through Sr. KG — with a team of 15+ dedicated, trained teachers who truly love what they do. Our Smart Digital Classrooms, Abacus Program, Biometric Attendance, CCTV Security, Monthly Parent Meetings, and Annual School Trips make us unlike any other pre-primary school in the region.

At Future School, we don't just teach — we build confident, curious, and creative young minds who are truly ready for the future.

  • Mission: To provide every child with a safe, modern, and joyful learning experience that builds a strong foundation for life.
  • Vision: To be Dhule's most trusted early childhood institution — raising confident, creative, and compassionate leaders of tomorrow.
  • Values: Love for every child, respect for individuality, creativity, discipline, safety, and a lifelong passion for learning.
